Accolades

Recognition by Congresswoman Jackie Speier

Upon retiring from the City Council in 2015, Congresswoman Jackie Speier presented to me a plaque noting that my name and accomplishments were recorded the U.S. House of Representatives Congressional Record.

The last Paragraph of the Recognition states the following:

"Mr. Speaker, I ask the House of Representatives to rise with me to honor a great man, public servant and good friend. Art Kiesel stands for integrity, commitment, and perseverance. He will be missed in Foster City's public life, but his contributions will make Foster City a stronger and more vibrant community for decades to come."

Resolution from the State Legislature

Resolution from the State Legislature from Assembly Member Kevin Mullin and State Senator Jerry Hill, presented in December, 2015.

The last Paragraph of the Resolution states the following:

"Resolved by Assembly Member Kevin Mullin and Senator Jerry Hill, That they take personal pleasure in commending the Honorable Art Kiesel upon the occasion of his retirement as a member of the Foster City City Council and Mayor of the City of Foster City, for his admirable record of responsible citizenship and dedicated service, and for enhancing the lives of the people of Foster City, and convey their warm best wishes for further achievement and personal satisfaction in the future."

Foster City Islander

Article Appearing in the Foster City Islander, ~Jan 2016

Closing paragraph:
"During his last meeting, former councilwoman and former Mayor Linda Koelling said that Kiesel will be remembered for his “integrity, professionalism, honesty, humbleness, selflessness, and being honorable.” We could not agree more. Kiesel was always willing to talk to all sides in a dispute and was consistently well prepared for every meeting. When he disagreed with others, he did it in a professional manner and was respectful to everyone who spoke before the Council. We are sorry to see Art leave office and wish him well in his future endeavours."
